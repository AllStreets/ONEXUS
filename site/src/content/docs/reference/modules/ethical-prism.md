## Overview

Ethical Prism -- multi-framework ethical analysis for high-stakes decisions.
Runs a decision through 7 ethical frameworks (Utilitarian, Deontological, Virtue Ethics, Care Ethics, Contractualist, Rights-Based, Pragmatic), then synthesizes where they agree, conflict, and what the tensions reveal. Does not recommend -- presents the landscape.

- **Version:** `1.0.0`
- **Class:** `EthicalPrismModule`
- **Module name:** `ethical_prism`
