## Overview

Symbiosis -- emergent neural pathways between modules.
Tracks which module chains produce successful outcomes and strengthens those connections over time. Analyzes routing history to identify strong collaborations, emerging pathways, and decaying connections.

- **Version:** `1.0.0`
- **Class:** `SymbiosisModule`
- **Module name:** `symbiosis`

## Tier
